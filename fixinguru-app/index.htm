About Handyfix App

FixingGuru is an app providing a platform that connects customers and merchants, making it easy for customers to find the right person for the job they need to be done.

Customers can post a job request – whether it’s something as simple as fixing a tap or installing lighting – and then merchants can bid on the job. This makes it easy for customers to compare prices and choose the merchant they’re most comfortable with.

Once a customer has chosen a fixer, they can chat within the platform to clarify any cost or requirements questions. And once the job is complete, FixingGuru takes a small percentage of the payment as commission.

People can be assured that they will get quality services at competitive prices when they use HandyFix.

This is because all service providers on the platform are vetted, and only those with a good track record are allowed to bid for work. In addition, businesses can view ratings and reviews other users leave before choosing a service provider.

This process makes it easy for customers to find the right person for the job. It makes it simpler and more affordable for fixers to find new customers. So why not give FixingGuru a try today?

How Does Handyfix Work?

Handyfix is the perfect solution for busy people who need help with everyday tasks. Our easy-to-use platform instantly connects you with skilled Fixers to help with cleaning, home repairs, personal shopping, running errands, and more.

01. POST

You describe the service you need, when and where you need it, and indicate a budget.

02. BID

Our Fixer community members then bid to get the job.

03. CHOOSE

You choose the Fixer you want to work with based on their qualifications, price, and reviews. Once you are confirmed and Fixer accepted your job , you can make payment through the app. It’s that simple!

04. COMPLETE

Fixer will go to the site and perform the task and complete it

05. REVIEW

Once the job is completed, you get Handyfix to release the payment to the fixer and write a review for the fixer . It’s that simple!
